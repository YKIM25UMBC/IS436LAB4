Welcome to the Department of Information Systems at UMBC

Our interdisciplinary program helps students develop in-demand skills, preparing them for careers in IT and IS. Located near technology hubs, our suburban campus is an exciting place to be for students in tech. With a variety of degree programs to choose from, we offer programs designed to give you options for applying information systems to your career.

Student Opportunities

The department uses a central form to collect research interests from our undergraduate and graduate students to help match students with projects supervised by IS faculty.

Visit our Current Opportunities webpage for complete information and details about internships, research positions, and career development programs.
